SCPPaymentIntent


@interface SCPPaymentIntent : NSObject <SCPJSONDecodable, NSCopying>

A PaymentIntent is an object that represents your intent to collect payment from a customer, tracking the lifecycle of the payment process through each step. Each PaymentIntent typically correlates with a single “cart” or customer session in your application.
